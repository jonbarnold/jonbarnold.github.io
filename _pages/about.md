Hi, I'm Jon, a mechanical engineer and Engineering Manager at [Janelia Research Campus](https://www.janelia.org/support-team/janelia-experimental-technology) (Howard Hughes Medical Institute) in Ashburn, VA.

I design tools, instruments, and systems that help scientists advance their research. My work spans mechanism design, motion control, and systems integration — from concept through fabrication. I have a particular interest in the intersection of precision mechanical design and scientific instrumentation.

Before Janelia, I developed products across a range of industries — confocal microscopes, lab automation equipment, medical diagnostic instruments, and factory equipment for the rail industry, including work on instruments that automate diagnostic testing for cervical cancer.

My strengths are in part, mechanism, and systems design. I use CAD as my primary tool and code where it helps.

Outside of work I enjoy cycling, volunteering at local parks, and working on design projects of my own.
